Our Mission

Established in memory of our beloved sister, wife, mother and daughter, Odunmolorun Osonuga, who passed away in 2024, our mission is simple yet profound: to alleviate the financial burden on new mothers and ensure they can access quality healthcare without the worry of hospital debts, and to provide support for women seeking IVF treatments.

We aim to make a positive impact on the lives of these women and their families, bringing joy and relief, while honoring our sister's compassionate spirit.

About The Odunmolorun Osonuga Foundation

Executive Summary

The Odunmolorun Osonuga Foundation is a non-profit organization established in memory of our beloved sister, wife, and daughter, Odunmolorun Osonuga, who passed away in 2024. Our mission is to provide financial assistance to women who deliver on November 27th and are unable to pay their hospital bills, and to offer support for In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) treatments. We aim to make a positive impact on the lives of these women and their families, while honoring our sister's memory.

Introduction

The Odunmolorun Osonuga Foundation is a response to the growing need for financial assistance among women facing childbirth costs and those pursuing fertility treatments. Our foundation aims to bridge the gap between these women and the financial resources they need to access quality healthcare and achieve their dreams of parenthood.

Goals and Objectives

  • Provide Post-Delivery Financial Assistance: To provide financial assistance to women who deliver on November 27th and are unable to pay their hospital bills.
  • Support IVF Treatments: To offer financial and informational support to individuals and couples pursuing In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) treatments.
  • Support Women in Need: To support women who are facing financial difficulties due to childbirth or fertility challenges, and to help them access necessary medical care.
  • Honor Our Sister's Memory: To honor our sister's memory by making a positive impact on the lives of women and their families.
  • Raise Awareness: To raise awareness about the challenges faced by women regarding childbirth costs and the financial burden of fertility treatments.

How We Help: Our Methodology

Our Approach to Post-Delivery Financial Assistance

For women delivering on November 27th who are unable to pay their hospital bills, our process is designed to be efficient, empathetic, and impactful.

01

Identification of Beneficiaries

We will work closely with hospitals and healthcare providers to identify women who deliver on November 27th and are genuinely unable to pay their hospital bills. This collaborative approach ensures we reach those most in need.

02

Application & Selection Process

A clear application and selection process will be established. This process will ensure transparency and fairness, helping us verify the financial need of potential beneficiaries.

03

Provision of Financial Assistance

Once selected, we will directly provide financial assistance to the beneficiaries, helping them settle their hospital debts and alleviate a significant burden during a crucial time.

Our Approach to IVF Assistance

For individuals and couples seeking In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) treatments, we aim to provide crucial support to help them achieve their dreams of starting a family.

01

Information & Guidance

We provide resources and guidance on understanding IVF procedures, clinics, and the overall journey, empowering individuals with knowledge.

02

Financial Support Assessment

Through a compassionate and confidential assessment, we determine eligibility for financial assistance towards IVF treatment costs.

03

Connecting & Assisting

We connect beneficiaries with reputable fertility clinics and provide direct financial aid to cover a portion of their IVF expenses, easing the financial burden.

Our Commitment

Every step of our methodology is guided by our commitment to making a tangible, positive impact on the lives of mothers and families, reflecting the spirit of compassion that inspired our foundation.

Partnerships

Collaborating for Greater Impact

The success of the Odunmolorun Osonuga Foundation hinges on strong collaborations. We believe that by working together with like-minded organizations, we can amplify our efforts and reach more women in need.

We actively seek to partner with:

  • Hospitals & Healthcare Providers: To facilitate the identification of beneficiaries for post-delivery assistance and streamline the payment process for hospital bills.
  • Fertility Clinics & Specialists: To collaborate on providing IVF assistance, ensuring access to quality care and support for individuals pursuing fertility treatments.
  • Other Non-Profit Organizations: To share resources, knowledge, and expand our reach through joint initiatives across all our programs.
